## Notes
* **Compliance vs. Validation:** Compliance is not "done" with SPDX; rather, SPDX supports the **validation** required for compliance.
* **The Roadmap:**
    1. Complete spreadsheet (identify key points of risk).
    2. Map against SPDX profiles (defined uniquely per company).
    3. Translate CRA issues into an engineering method example.
* **Strategy:** Map key issues (starting with the product), followed by a strategy to map against SPDX. The product example should provide guidance across multiple product categories.
* **CRA Evidence:** Discussion on allowances for evidence presentation. Use of **bundles** is recommended.
* **Methodology:** CRA is creating content on methodology use. There is a gap in business understanding regarding how SPDX can be used to capture, share, and mitigate risk.
* **Industry Gap:** Software engineering is perceived as decades behind mission-critical hardware (e.g., aerospace) regarding security and reliability.
