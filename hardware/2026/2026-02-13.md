## Notes

### Roles & The CRA

* **Context:** The Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) heavily emphasizes roles within the supply chain and user-focused risk assessments.
* **Requirement Creation:** This was identified as the key to defining role qualifications.
* **Usability:** Felix Reichmann raised the concern: *Will people actually know how to use SPDX?*

### Personas vs. Roles

* **Definitions:**
* **Persona:** Defined by capabilities and "needs." A persona can play multiple roles.
* **Role:** A specific functional position.


* **Data Host Persona:** Reviewed the CEBok definition; a persona is not just a qualification, but an entity with the "ability" to provide or fulfill.
* **Wants vs. Needs:**
* **Wants:** Aspirational expectations.
* **Needs:** Concrete requirements based on those wants.
* **Recommendation:** Use `Tracetodetails` for "wants" to differentiate them from core "needs."



### Requirement Logic

* Differentiation is required between "shall" and "want" to prevent confusion.
* At the point of resolution, all conflicts between stakeholders and the system must be decided.
* **The Logic Chain:** Decisions $\rightarrow$ Requirements $\rightarrow$ Choices.
