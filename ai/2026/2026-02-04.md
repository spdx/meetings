#### **Ontology & Roles (The "Modeling Gap")**

  * **Ontology Foundations:** Mitre D3FEND uses [Common Core Ontologies (CCO)](https://github.com/CommonCoreOntology/CommonCoreOntologies/), built on **Basic Formal Ontology (BFO)** ([ISO standard](https://www.iso.org/standard/74572.html)).
  * **CoSAI Links:** CoSAI risk definition is technical; see [COSAI risk map](https://github.com/cosai-oasis/secure-ai-tooling/issues/136).
  * **Role Discussion:**
      * **Bob/Steven:** Need use cases for stakeholders/personas to satisfy **CRA compliance**.
      * **Raymond:** Common roles are required to facilitate discussion.
      * **Harmonization:** Roles exist in Build, Supply Chain, Security, Ops, and Functional Safety. If they intersect, they must be resolved across profiles.
      * **Alfred:** Suggested using a "creation" concept for roles for consistency across the ontology.
  * **Proposal:** Use CCO/BFO as a base for generic supply chain entities (roles/personas) in Core/SupplyChain; AI profile can then specialize.
  * **Modeling the World:** SPDX was not originally born to be a "model of the world" ontology, but rather to document things happening in the world. The current "gap" results from trying to use SPDX for broader world-modelling.
  * **Persona Scopes:** \* **Build:** Focuses on life cycle scopes (who is in each scope).
      * **Supply Chain:** Focuses on `Action` and `DefinedProcess` (who takes the action).
      * **Data Creation:** May need to be separate from the standard Supply Chain set.
