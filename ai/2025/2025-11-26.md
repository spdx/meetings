- For generic AI terms, propose using ones from ISO/IEC 22989:2022 (free to download):
  https://www.iso.org/standard/74296.html
  - **artificial intelligence, AI**: “research and development of mechanisms and applications of AI systems”
  - **artificial intelligence system, AI system**: “engineered system that generates outputs such as content, forecasts, recommendations or decisions for a given set of human-defined objectives”
  - **autonomy, autonomous**: “characteristic of a system that is capable of modifying its intended domain of use or goal without external intervention, control or oversight”
  - **automatic, automation, automated**: “pertaining to a process or system that, under specified conditions, functions without human intervention”
