#### Element inlining rules / CreationInfo exception in JSON-LD serialization
- https://github.com/spdx/spdx-3-model/issues/1104
- Keep for 3.1.
- Need to consolidate the serialization into just the spec repo.
- Inlining is not what any of the implementers are doing.
- More flexibility in inlining for other properties is supported by other tools.
- Recommendation to update documentation to reflect what tools are doing.
- Inlining would break some tools.
- Elements and subclasses of elements cannot be inlined, but others may be.
- To be taken up in the Implementers call and resolved there.
